Been doing this
my whole life

Started as an audio engineer, transitioned into DJing in the early 2000s, and never looked back. Known for high-energy sets that blend hip-hop, R&B, reggaeton, afrobeats, amapiano, and global club sounds.

The ability to read a room and seamlessly mix genres has made Sinatra a favorite among celebrities, brands, and international nightlife audiences. From NYC's legendary scene to residencies in Tokyo — the sound travels.
