Biography

Brooklyn-born DJ Sinatra is one of the most sought-after nightlife DJs in the world, known for high-energy sets that blend hip-hop, R&B, reggaeton, afrobeats, amapiano, and global club sounds. With a career rooted in New York City's legendary nightlife scene, Sinatra built his reputation as a DJ who knows how to move a crowd and create unforgettable moments on the dance floor.

Originally starting his career as an audio engineer, Sinatra transitioned into DJing in the early 2000s and quickly became a fixture in some of the most influential clubs and events around the world. His ability to read a room and seamlessly mix genres has made him a favorite among celebrities, brands, and international nightlife audiences alike.

Throughout his career, DJ Sinatra has performed at exclusive events and after-parties for artists and brands including Bruno Mars, Miley Cyrus, and Dolce & Gabbana, as well as major celebrations like the New York Yankees' 2009 World Series after-party. His global presence has taken him from New York to destinations across Europe, Asia, and beyond.

Now based between New York and Tokyo, Sinatra serves as a resident DJ at 1 OAK Tokyo, where his signature sound continues to bring together international crowds and nightlife culture from around the world.

Whether performing in elite clubs, private celebrity events, or global nightlife destinations, DJ Sinatra delivers a sound and experience that represents the energy of Brooklyn, the style of New York, and the pulse of the international dance floor.
